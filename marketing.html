Meet Cornileus

An AI counselor that actually knows your numbers.

Cornileus is a financial counselor built into Ascend. He combines fee-only-CFP planning instincts with index-fund discipline — and he sees your full snapshot, so his answers reference your real situation.

  • Builds full multi-phase roadmaps with one prompt
  • Surfaces spending leaks before you overspend further
  • Detects untracked recurring charges
  • Captures wins, logs deposits, completes goals — does the work
  • 13 action verbs. Pin any reply to keep forever.
