What's inside

Everything that matters,
in one app.

Most apps do one thing. Ascend covers the parts of your life that all influence each other — so you actually see the whole picture.

✅

Daily habits

Streaks, groups (health, mind, faith), and a clean checklist that actually feels good to use. No notifications guilting you.

🎯

Goals you finish

Short-term sprints chained to your 5-year vision. The AI helps break big goals into next-week-sized moves.

💰

Money in plain English

Safe-to-spend today, budget vs. actuals, debt payoff with avalanche/snowball, optional bank sync via Plaid.

📈

Stocks & investing

4-pillar analysis, Monte Carlo projections, scan stocks/REITs/crypto/bonds for buys. Built-in StockAnalyzer.

🏠

Housing & mortgages

Buyer's vs. seller's market by ZIP, current 30y/15y rates, full payment calculator with PMI and refi math.

✨

AI coach

3-minute setup chat builds your starter plan. Monthly insights read your spending and tell you what to fix.

How it works

Three minutes to set up.
Yours forever.

01

Talk to Ascend AI

Quick conversational onboarding — your name, the area you want to improve, your 5-year vision. AI builds the starter plan.

Tweak & track

03

See it compound

Daily streaks, monthly insights, year-in-review. The numbers do the convincing — discipline becomes the easy choice.
